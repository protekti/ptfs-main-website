Beginners Info

This Page Will Tell You All About Setting Up For A Roleplay As A Pilot Or ATC

 

Pilot Beginers Info

Step 1.

Firstly You Need To Join The Discord Server By Clicking The Button In The Navbar.

Step 2.

Wait For SSU in #ssu.

Step 3.

Once A SSU has been anounced fill in your flight plan. Send This To ATC:

Callsign : (your ptfs callsign)
Departure airport : (your takeoff airport)
Arrival airport : (your landing airport)
Departure Time : (departue time in zulu time)
Estimated Flight Time : (estimated flight time)
Current Gate : (gate that you have spawned in)
Expected Gate : (gate that you expect to stop at)



Step 4.

Contacting Tower. To contact tower join the Voice Call of your airport (if your airport is not there then go to the OTHER voice call).

Step 5. Setting up. To Do ANYTHING You Need To Request IFR Clearance By Saying:

(Tower Name), Good Afternoon (Callsign), At Gate (gate number), Requesting IFR Clearance to (your destination).

Tower Then Will Say Some Stuff REMEMBER IT. Then Once Tower Has Stopped Speaking Then Reat EVERYTHING Tower Said.

Step 6.

Requesting Pushback/Engine Start. From Now On It Is Going To Be Easy. To Request Pushback say:

This Is (callsign) Requesting Push And Start.

If Tower Replies:

(callsign) You Are Cleared To Push And Start.

Then Turn On Your Engines And Pushback.

If Tower Replies:

Standby.

Then Wait For ATC to clear you for push and start.

Step 7.

Getting To The Runway. To Get To The Runway Say (callsign) Requesting Taxi To (pick a Runway)

If Tower Replies:

Standby.

Then Wait For ATC to clear you to taxi to (runway).

If Tower Replies:

(callsign) You Are Cleared To Taxi To Runway Via (letters).

Then Open The Taxiways Of Your airport they are in #usefull insfo. Then Find The Route For Example The Letters Are D A D4 E E4 then I find those letters and I connect them one by one from the first letter to end.

Step 8.

Taking Off. To Takeoff Contact Tower And Say:

(callsign) Requesting Takeoff From Runway(Runway)

And Again If The Answer Is Standby Then Wait Otherwise Takeoff

Step 9.

Landing.To land Say To Tower:

(callsign) Requesting To Land On Runway (pick a runway number)

If Tower Responds Standby then wait Otherwise Land On That Runway.

Step 10.

Taxing To Gate. To Taxi To Gate Say To The Tower:

(callsign)Requesting To Taxi To Gate(your gate that you selected whilst setting up the flight plan)

If Tower Responds Standby then wait Otherwise Taxi To That Gate Via The Letters Givens.
